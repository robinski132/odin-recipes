Arancini

Description

Usually served as an appetizer but it makes a great late-night snack too. Hearty and delicious risotto-balls. The Italians call them "Aruncini".

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups (200g) almond flour or very finely ground almonds, sifted
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1/4 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon almond extract
