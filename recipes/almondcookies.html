Almond Cookies

Description

The perfect little treat. Light, sweet and chewy. Some of the best cookies in existence!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups (200g) almond flour or very finely ground almonds, sifted
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1/4 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon almond extract
